 step 1 - Wash yout hands and the mince chicken properly.
 step 2 - In a large bowl mix together 1 cup of All purpose flour and 1/2 cup of water until it forms a dough. Cover the dough and let rest. 
 step 3 - For the filling mix together 1 cup of mince chicken, 1 cup of green onions, 1 and 1/2 teaspoon of chilli powder, 1 tsp of garlic powder. 
 step 4 - Roll out the dough and knead it till it is 1 cm thick. Cut out circles.
 step 5 - In the circles, fill a spoonful of the filling you made and close it in whatever shape you like. 
 step 6 - When many dumplings are ready, Start adding the dumpling in a pan full of 1/4 cup of water + AP flour mixture, let them cook for 5 min.
 

 Take them out and serve hot !!
